A Brief Journey Through Time

Origins of Chess

Chess began as Chaturanga in ancient India and evolved into Shatranj in Persia. The game spread through the Islamic world and into Europe, laying the foundation for the modern version we know today.

Development of Modern Chess

In Europe, chess rules were standardized, and the game gained popularity in royal courts and among intellectuals. The powerful queen piece was introduced, and the first formal chess tournaments began in this period.

Chess in the Modern Era

Chess became a global sport with the first World Chess Championship in 1886. The 20th century saw legendary matches and the rise of chess engines. Today, online platforms have made chess accessible to millions worldwide.
