History of the Queen

The Queen evolved from the ancient game of Chaturanga, where it had limited movement. Over time, especially in Europe, the Queen gained its iconic abilities: moving horizontally, vertically, and diagonally. It became the powerhouse of the game, capable of controlling the board and attacking from all directions.

How the Queen Moves

The Basic Movement

The Queen moves as far as possible in any direction: horizontally, vertically, and diagonally. This gives it immense power and flexibility, allowing it to control large sections of the board.
